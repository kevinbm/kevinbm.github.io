About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University, working with Prof. Jean Honorio. I am broadly interested in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. My research focuses on developing algorithms that are computationally and statistically efficient for various machine learning problems. Recently, I have worked on structured prediction problems with latent variables.

Previously, I received a BSc in Mechatronics Engineering from the National University of Engineering in Lima, Peru where I was advised by Alberto Coronado.
